⬡ Platform features

Everything you need to run events at scale

From creation to certificate generation — the complete toolkit for professional event management.

🔐

Role-Based Authentication

Secure multi-user auth powered by Supabase. Separate admin and participant flows, with session management and row-level security.

📡

Live Event Rooms

Real-time event rooms where participants can join, see who's attending, and engage — all synchronized across multiple users simultaneously.

📱

QR Code Attendance

Every participant gets a unique QR token. Admins scan to mark attendance instantly. Zero friction, zero paper, real-time tracking.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Visual analytics: registration trends, attendance rates, feedback scores, rating distributions — all presented in beautiful, interactive charts.

⭐

Structured Feedback

Collect post-event ratings and written feedback from participants. View aggregate scores and individual responses in your admin dashboard.

🏆

Certificate Generator

Auto-generate branded participation certificates with unique cert IDs. Participants download instantly as high-resolution PNG — no design tool needed.

⬡ Process

From zero to event in minutes

No configuration hell. No backend setup. Sign up and ship.

01

Create account

Sign up as an Admin or Participant. Your role determines what you can do in the platform.

02

Create your event

Admins publish events with details, capacity, category, and location. Open it when you're ready.

03

Participants join

Users browse open events, register with one click, and receive their unique QR attendance token.

04

Go live & track

Scan QR codes at the door, collect feedback after, issue certificates, and review your analytics.
